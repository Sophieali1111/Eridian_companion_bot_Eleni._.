# Eridian_companion_bot_Eleni._. 
WATCHED PROJECT HAIL MARRY and wanted my own Eridian bot ROCKY ! Meet Eleni [ torch in Greek].

# What is this?
A robot has 5 legs and at least 6 degrees of freedom, has a OLED showing cute faces, temp and custom information. Can be linked to a controller but also could just be a desk top buddy with automatic responses to temp change, tap on the head and time. Would make your life a bit more cozy and whimsy:)))


# How to form bond with Eleni? [how to use]
Eleni can connect to mobile with its microo controller(ESP32DEV),It can tell you weather stats such as tempreture, moister, and also tell you time, date, weekday via OLED screen. Screen switches to the diffrent stats with a headpat (touchpad mounted on top). Eleni expresses emotion with OLED animated face she is pretty cute ^_^ .She can move around a lot of places up and down terrain, You can kindly ask(control) her with a remote on your phone or hard code her to do certain things.
