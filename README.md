# Eridian_companion_bot_Eleni._. 
WATCHED PROJECT HAIL MARRY and wanted my own Eridian bot ROCKY ! Meet Eleni [ torch in Greek].

# What is this?
A robot has 5 legs and at least 6 degrees of freedom, has a OLED showing cute faces, temp and custom information. Can be linked to a controller but also could just be a desk top buddy with automatic responses to temp change, tap on the head and time. Would make your life a bit more cozy and whimsy:)))

# Features
- move through terrain
- Cute animated bitmoji
- tempreture and moisture
- react to headpats
- remote controlable (via iphone)

# How to form bond with Eleni? [how to use]
Eleni can connect to mobile with its microo controller(ESP32DEV),It can tell you weather stats such as tempreture, moister- from DTH 22 modoule, and also tell you time, date, weekday via OLED screen. Screen switches to the diffrent stats with a headpat (touchpad mounted on top). Eleni expresses emotion with OLED animated face she is pretty cute ^_^ .She can move around a lot of places up and down terrain, You can kindly ask(control) her with a remote on your phone or hard code her to do certain things.
